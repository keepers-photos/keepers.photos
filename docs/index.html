In a few simple steps, go from overwhelm to overjoy!

Train You teach the AI what each family member looks like over the years. It will automatically tag each photo for you

Collect Organise photos into collections, organised by family member, special occasion, place and activity

Make Enjoy, create and share. Create photo albums and prints with a few clicks.

One more thing ...

The privacy of your photos and the longevity of the service should not be an afterthought.

The team behind Keepers are all parents. We want to make sure that the service and your photos are around for a long time. We appreciate how vulnerable it might feel to choose a new service to organise your family's most cherished memories.

We make money when you purchase prints and books through the app. We will never sell your data to third parties. We will never use your photos for any other purpose than to help you organise them.

We plan to register as a Benefit Corporation. We believe it provides the legal framework to build a long-lasting company that stays true to our mission of being the guardian for your family memories.

Until recently, the idea of a for-profit company pursuing social good at the expense of shareholder value had no clear protection under U.S. corporate law. Benefit Corporations are created to oblige for-profit companies to consider the impact of their decisions on society, not only shareholders.

We commit to contribute at least 1% of our annual sales to environmental causes through 1% for the planet. Our children will inherit a world that is dramatically different from us. By supporting the preservation and restoration of the natural environment. we do our humble parts in tackling our planet's most pressing environmental issues.

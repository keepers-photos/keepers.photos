Are your photos out of control?

It probably didn't start off this way.

At the start you sorted some, printed and shared others, and maybe even filed some into albums

As the kids grow, the pile of photos grows too. And the mountain starts to be unwieldy and more unorganised.

Scrolling to find a photo takes what seems like an eternity, and often you can't find it, it's stressful!

Then mix in work photos, memes and screenshots. Sorting is now an impossible task. With little time to sort and no system to use you're stuck!

At Keepers, we set out to change this.

We take care of the time-consuming and tedious tagging and filing and have even built an AI to identify each child as they grow.

After a super quick setup you can easily and instantly access, curate and relive the moments and remember the joy.

How it works

In a few simple steps, go from overwhelm to overjoy!

Curate Keepers automatically finds your family photos. It filters out screenshots, memes and identifies similar shots

Train You teach our AI to what each family member looks like over the years so you don't have to manually tag each photo

Collect Organise photos into collections, organised by family member, special occasion, place and activity

Make Enjoy, create and share, effortlessly. Create photo albums and prints with a few clicks.

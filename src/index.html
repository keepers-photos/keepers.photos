Frequent Asked Questions

How about my live photos and videos?

Keepers will allow you to use Live Photos as still photos. iOS' Photos will automatically pick a frame to represent it, which we use as the photo. Unfortunately, we do not support videos at this time.

Do you offer hard cover books or lay flat books?

Not yet. To ensure the best overall experience, we are focusing on the soft cover books right now. We are talking to some of the world's best hard cover manufacturers at the moment but we don't have a firm date to share just yet.

How do I get in contact with you?

Please reach us at hello@keepers.photos. We are a small company and nothing delights us more than hearing from our customers. We'll get back to you within 24 hours. Rain or shine.
